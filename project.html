This report was a group project that I did a few years ago. It looked into the root causes of the prosperity of real estate market in Mainland China. Some reasons that could be quantified were identified using fundamental economic theories and some statistical techniques. For those who were qualitative, we referred to a number of theses to support our ideas. Apart from the identification of the reasons, we also attempted to provide some recommendations as mitigation to the overheated market.
