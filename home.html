Welcome!
Thank you for reviewing my portfolio. I am a current student studying computer programming at Humber College in Toronto, Canada.
Background
Before attending at Humber College, I obtained my bachelor degree in my home city and worked for a couple of years in banking industry. My previous work required me to involve a number of aspects including trading, credit analysis and coding. Handling the enormous amount of work enabled me to understand what role programming skills could play in our real life and how it could make our tasks easier. Therefore, I decided to further my study in programming.
Career Goals
The exposure I gained in the previous work experience allowed me to gain a more thorough understanding of the industry and also inspired me how programming skills would be helpful in handling a great deal of data and making decisions. I would like to apply the techniques I will acquire to my job in the future so that I could provide a more logical advice in a fast-paced environment and be able to identify new trading opportunities.
